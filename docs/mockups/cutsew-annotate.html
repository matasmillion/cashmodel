Draw on the photos — red box & red text

A small Annotate button on any call-out photo opens this editor. You can drop a red box (move it, resize it from the corners, and rotate it to any angle with the knob on top) and red text (move it, click to type). Marks are saved on the photo and show in the page 07/08 preview. The numbered-dot tool, the card layout, the BOM and the PDF are all left exactly as they are.

How it looks once saved

Each call-out photo gets a small Annotate tab. After you draw and hit Done, the red box and text show right on the card and a small count badge appears. The very same marks show on the big page 07/08 live preview.

This works on every call-out photo on pages 07 & 08 — the big garment reference and all the detail close-ups (main and supporting).

Stays put when you come back: every mark is saved as a position on the photo (e.g. “starts 16% from the left”), filed under that photo's slot on the Cut & Sew block — so it lands in the same spot no matter the screen size.  ·  Always matches: because the marks live on the one shared block, the same red box/text appear in the library card and in every Style tech pack that borrowed that block; edit it in either place and both update.  ·  Added: only this overlay.   Untouched: the numbered-dot tool, the call-out layout, the BOM, and the PDF.
